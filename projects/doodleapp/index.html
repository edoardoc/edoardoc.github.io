The shared canvas workflow

  1. Connect both devices to the same Wi‑Fi network, hotspot, or ad-hoc LAN.
  2. Open DoodleTag and tap Start Shared Canvas.
  3. Let glue mode auto-discover your partner or read them the pairing code.
  4. Sketch together with synchronized layers, colors, brush settings, and smiles.

DoodleTag needs everyone on the same local network. Internet is optional—the app rides LAN broadcasting to keep every doodle perfectly in sync.
