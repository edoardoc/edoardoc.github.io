Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2025

Doodle Tag (com.u500nits.DoodleTag) ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how our app handles information.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (e.g., name, email address).
  • No user registration or login data.
  • No usage analytics or tracking.
  • No server-side storage of your drawings.

Local Network Connectivity

Doodle Tag enables collaborative drawing via your local network (e.g., Wi-Fi). All drawing data is transmitted only over your local network and is not sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not targeted at children under 4. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 4.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of changes by updating the Effective Date above.
